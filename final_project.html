Title: The Legend of Neotopia

On another day with Computus Progremi, the Artificial Intelligence popped up to see how I am doing. I decided to hear another story from Computus. Computus Progremi told me a story about a hidden utopian country located on Earth. Neotopia has tons of scientific discoveries, technological advancements, and fascinating innovations. Diverse races coexist. Neotopia makes it easier to find 5-star housing, companies, restaurants, etc. Neotopia is like a fantastic dream city most people would love living in. It has sci-fi/fantasy and world-building vibes. The lore for Neotopia can be one of the ideas for an anime about the Mysteries of Life.
