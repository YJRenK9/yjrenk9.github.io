Title: Blue Ghost and the Randomized Ghost Gang


My project 2 uses my avatar from project 1, which is a blue ghost. When it comes to a randomizer project, I think about how I can incorporate my avatar with project 2. An idea came to mind and suggested me to display randomized ghosts like seeing each ghost collection at a time. Coming up with this idea made me think about the types of personalities we see that’s part of life.
There are 14 ghosts that are displayed randomly. Each have unique colors and personalities. Let’s see if you can find all of them. There are two supposedly unique looking ghosts: one says “LOL, you’ve been pranked!” and the other says “Muni Muni Muni” while nomming on a lunar edition mochi-cake.
