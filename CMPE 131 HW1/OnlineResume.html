Short Biography

Hello there, my name is Jordan Le. I am a Computer Engineering Major with a Studio Art Minor. But, you can think of me as a Software Engineering student who's minoring in Digital Media Art. I have a goal to become a software engineer. I am willing to do what it takes to reach my goal. I choose this career because I believe I can create things and make life better for people. Before going to university, I had some prior programming and digital art experiences. Most of that came from learning the skills outside of school. As I progress through SJSU, I learned Object-Oriented Programming and Data Structures. Java and C++ are the programming languages I studied the most as an SJSU student. I also learned more about HTML/CSS when taking a Digital Media Art class. I figured this was something I genuinely enjoy doing for my career. It became my passion. I realized how software applies to two things: technical and artistic. With that, the possibilities of using computer software can be endless.
